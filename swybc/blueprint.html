Institution Architecture

Three Pillars

SWYBC operates as a three-pillar institution — each pillar generates independent revenue and feeds the others.

Pillar I
The Classic
Annual international tournament. 14U / 16U / 18U. 12 nations. Pool play at SoCal D1 university facilities. Championship Weekend at Dodger Stadium (2027+). Primary revenue engine and brand builder.
Pillar II
WCPI
World Classic Performance Institute. Elite Academy + Summer Intensives + digital subscription platform. Recurring revenue. Activates year-round between Classic events.
Pillar III
WCET
World Classic Exchange Tours. USA→Japan outbound + inbound Asian national teams. High-margin premium product. Builds international federation relationships needed for The Classic.
2026 Focus100% of 2026 resources go toward Pillar I — the Christmas Classic. WCPI and WCET are architecture-level decisions for post-Christmas planning.
