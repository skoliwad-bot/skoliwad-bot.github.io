The Strategy — Path B

Don't launch small. Build toward the full international event. Use the Christmas Classic 2026 as a proof-of-concept to validate operations, build relationships, generate real revenue, and create the credibility needed to approach prospective partners and federations before the August 2027 International Classic.

The Christmas Classic is also the only national team tryout — selection committee evaluates all players across 4 days. Three national teams announced at the Dec 29 closing ceremony. The August 2027 International Classic and Dodger Stadium venue are revealed at the same ceremony.

Why December 2026 Works

Cash flow timing: Tryout fees ($100/player × 1,296 players = $129,600) collected October/November — the event is cash flow positive before a single Christmas Classic game is played.

Kyrin's eligibility window: Born 6/11/2012. He is 14U through September 1, 2027. The August 2027 International Classic is his last eligible 14U event — the one we're building the entire institution toward.

Operational proof: Running 57 games across 4 days at D1 university facilities proves the operational model to prospective partners, federations, and sponsors before any real capital is committed to the international event.

Institution Architecture

Three Pillars

SWYBC operates as a three-pillar institution — each pillar generates independent revenue and feeds the others.

Pillar I
The Classic
Annual international tournament. 14U / 16U / 18U. 12 nations. Pool play at SoCal D1 university facilities. Championship Weekend at Dodger Stadium (2027+). Primary revenue engine and brand builder.
Pillar II
WCPI
World Classic Performance Institute. Elite Academy programming + Summer Intensives + digital subscription platform for elite youth players. Recurring revenue. Activates year-round between Classic events.
Pillar III
WCET
World Classic Exchange Tours. USA→Japan outbound tours + inbound Asian national teams. High-margin premium product. Builds international federation relationships needed for the Classic.
2026 Focus 100% of 2026 resources go toward Pillar I — the Christmas Classic. WCPI and WCET are architecture-level decisions for post-Christmas planning. Do not distract from the proof-of-concept.
